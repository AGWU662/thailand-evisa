Frequently Asked Questions

How long does visa processing take? ‚ñº
Most visa applications are processed within 3-5 business days. However, processing times may vary depending on the type of visa and current application volume.
What documents do I need? ‚ñº
Required documents include: valid passport, recent photograph, flight itinerary, hotel booking, bank statement, and employment letter. Check the Documents Center for the complete checklist.
Can I track my application status? ‚ñº
Yes! Use the Track Application feature with your booking number to see real-time updates on your visa application status.
What payment methods are accepted? ‚ñº
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments (Bitcoin, USDT, Ethereum).
Can I modify my application after submission? ‚ñº
Once submitted, applications cannot be modified. However, you can contact support for assistance with urgent changes before processing begins.
